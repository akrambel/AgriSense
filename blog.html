Smart Farming with Advanced Weather Station

Empower your farm with precise, real-time data from our advanced weather station. From temperature and humidity to soil moisture and rain detection, optimize your agricultural practices with insights that enhance productivity and sustainability.

Key Features

Temperature Monitoring

Accurately measures temperature to help manage crop health and optimize growing conditions.

Humidity Tracking

Tracks humidity levels to prevent crop diseases and ensure ideal growing environments.

Pressure Sensing

Monitors atmospheric pressure to forecast weather changes and protect crops from adverse conditions.

Heat Index Calculation

Calculates the heat index to assess the combined effects of temperature and humidity on crops

Rain Detection

Detects rainfall in real-time to assist in irrigation planning and water management.

Altitude Measurement

Measures altitude to understand elevation-related factors that impact crop growth.

Soil Moisture Sensing

Monitors soil moisture levels to optimize irrigation and conserve water resources.
