This chart shows the annual crop yield from 2019 to 2023. Soil Moisture Development Over Time

This chart tracks soil moisture levels over a six-month period. Soil moisture is a critical factor in crop health, and maintaining optimal levels can prevent both over-irrigation and drought stress. By monitoring soil moisture trends, you can adjust irrigation schedules to ensure that crops receive the right amount of water, improving water efficiency and crop yield.
